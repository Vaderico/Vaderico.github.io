About me

My Story

My programming journey began when I wanted to create a mod for Minecraft. As I sat at my computer all day and night watching thenewboston's java tutorials, one after another light bulbs and switches were turning on in my head. I love programming, I realised, and I am good at it. Having walked my path of only feeling second best at everything, I started getting butterflies at the thought of becoming a professional in something. The following day I approached my local university, inquiring about studying programming. Fast forward two years and here I am as a second year Software Engineering student, driven to conquer this world of computers.

My Goals

I have a variety of dreams, but have a very clear goal. Technology is what drives the world, and software is what drives the technology. I want to run a programming school for children and teenagers, because adolescents need to break misconception that coding is hard. I also want to run my own software engineering company, building products that entertain and help every-day users. These things that I want to do in life are not too far away, I just need to work hard enough to achieve them.
